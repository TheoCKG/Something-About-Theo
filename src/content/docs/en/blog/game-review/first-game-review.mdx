> This sample article can be used as a review template.

## 1. How I Found This Game

- How did you first hear about it?
- Why did you decide to try it?

## 2. First Impressions

- **Visual style**: art direction, color, interface.
- **Music and sound**: whether they support the mood.
- **Learning curve**: how friendly the early experience is.

## 3. Favorite Moments

Pick two or three memorable scenes, mechanics, or encounters and explain why they stayed with you.

## 4. Friction Points

- Pacing issues, weak onboarding, balancing problems, or missed potential.

## 5. Overall Take

- What kind of game is this to you?
- Who would you recommend it to?
