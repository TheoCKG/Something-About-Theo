> This is a starter template for your first film review.

## 1. Film Information

- Title
- Director / Writer
- Main cast
- Genre and runtime

## 2. Spoiler-Free Impression

- What kind of audience is it for?
- What is the general tone?

## 3. Story and Theme

- What is the setting?
- What does the protagonist want?
- What theme do you think the film is really exploring?

## 4. Character and Performance

- Which character affected you most, and why?
- Did any performance significantly elevate or weaken the film for you?

## 5. Audiovisual Language and Detail

- Camera work, editing rhythm, music, sound, recurring imagery, or symbolism.

## 6. Personal Resonance

- What personal experience or reflection did this film connect to?
- What line, image, or moment stayed in your mind afterward?
