> This is a template for periodic learning reflection.

## 1. What Am I Learning?

- The topic itself.
- Why I started learning it.

## 2. What Was Difficult?

- Concepts that were hard to understand.
- Documentation that felt overwhelming.
- Hands-on attempts that did not work well at first.

## 3. How I Broke Through

- Switched materials or tutorials.
- Chose a better practice target.
- Asked others, searched discussions, or read source code.

## 4. Reusable Principles

- Build a minimal demo first, then deepen the details.
- When a concept feels abstract, find where it lives inside a real problem.

## 5. Next Steps

- What practice or small project comes next?
- Which direction deserves more time?
